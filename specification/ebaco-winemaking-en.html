Abstract

The E-BACO ontology defines a precise vocabulary for representing knowledge in the enology domain.

The E-BACO-Core module contains the basic concepts, properties and relations for describing wines.

It has been developped by the Complutense university of Madrid and the San Pablo CEU university.
